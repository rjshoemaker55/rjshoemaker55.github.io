About Me

Hello, my name is Raymond (RJ) Shoemaker. I am 23 years old and am currently attending the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp full stack web development program. On this website, you can find a few of the projects I have created and contributed to.

I first developed an interest in programming while doing Scratch projects in middle school. I've always enjoyed being able to control the looks and movements of the elements, and began exploring coding in my free time.

My interest in coding drove me to apply to this bootcamp. I am excited to begin my career in web development and transform what used to be a hobby into a creative buisness opportunity! If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to me on my contact form!
